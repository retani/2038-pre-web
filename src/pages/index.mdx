<p>
Today, in the year 2038, we have mastered the great crises. It was close, but we made it. The global economic and ecological disasters of the 2020s brought people, states, institutions and companies together. They jointly created an adaptable, self-sustaining system that governs globally, while at the same time giving decentralized local communities the space to maintain their individual way of living: an intelligent, social contract.
</p>

<p>
New technologies and Big Data helped us to turn new and old ideas of living together into reality. And often architects were part of the solutions because they had answers instead of coming up with more questions. Drama is now history. We live in a radical democracy, a radical bureaucracy. In a society that doesn't know or need heroines or villains.
</p>
